Even though it may be a rare occurrence, anything is possible to happen to the item once dispatched. To provide reassurance and reliance with our trusted couriers, we ask that every buyer obtains a receipt upon drop off and KEEP this receipt until the item has been delivered. If a parcel were to go missing, as long as you provide us the receipt and raise the query to us within the acceptable time (two business days), we can then use this receipt and communicate directly with the couriers to launch an investigation. We make it our goal to ensure all investigations are looked at thoroughly and fairly. Due to the depth of internal investigation required for most couriers when there’s a parcel issue, it will usually be resolved within 10 business days. More information about this can be found without our Terms and Conditions. If you have any issues relating to a package in transit, please email us at info@omnisnco.com or use our live chat 24/7 service. Please provide as much detail as possible.

If your item is reflecting as “delivered” but never arrived, we ask that you please reach out to us within two business days to ensure you are eligible to make a claim. If you have made changes to your delivery options through a carrier management system, this may void your eligibility to make a claim. However, we will assess each case to the best of our ability to ensure all users receive a fair outcome.

Please contact Omnis at info@omnisnco.com or visit our 24/7 Live Chat assistance, provide us your order number and explain the tracking issue you’re facing, in as much detail as possible.

If you have received your item but it is damaged, we will launch an investigation. In order for us to assess the situation, we will require photo(s) of the damage you’re referring to (sneakers and/or boxing). We have implemented a system where buyers must take several photos during the packaging phase and upload it to the appropriate section on their account. This is done to ensure the correct and compliant packaging standards were met. If no photos were taken by the Seller and an issue has been raised from the Buyer, this could result in a full refund to the Buyer.

We encourage all sellers to use our partnered couriers to ensure an efficient and timely delivery on your item. We will not be held responsible or liable for packages organised by third party carriers.

The first step that we would recommend you take is reaching out to the exact location you dropped your item to. This would provide you more information and insight its current status. Your item should still be at that location as there have been no physical scans of it elsewhere.

Shipping costs cover the expense of getting the item from the Seller to the Buyer. These costs will be included in the price breakdown when you place a bid or Buy Now. All rates below are based on the average weighting and sizes of a show box. Some sneakers will naturally be heavier, these will incur additional shipping charges. We ship with DHL and many more.

Congratulations on selling your first item! Once a transaction has been complete, your shipping details including the deadline can be found in the confirmation email we send once your item has been successfully purchased. This can also be found in your My Account section (My account > Selling). You must ensure you print out the shipping label and ship the item (adhering to our shipping standard procedure) prior to the deadline commencing. Most sneakers will have a deadline of two business days to be dispatched, some will have 3-5 depending on the type of release and the brand shipping nature.

Shipping Instructions Please ensure you follow these instructions or your payment will be delayed. As a reminder, Omnis is not responsible for any items not packaged correctly and securely. Place this invoice inside the box with the product Place the box into a bigger sized box that is no more than 5cm of your item from each side. Use bubble wrap or packing paper to ensure your item is secure and will not sustain damage during transit. Seal the package across the side flaps and the seams that run through the middle on the top and bottom surface. Do not use masking tape or duct tape. Tape or place the Omnis provided DHL label to the outside of your shipping box Drop off your sealed package to an authorised DHL location Ensure you get a RECEIPT which will allow you to keep track of your item during transit from drop off to delivery.
