FAQ

What is two-step verification?

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) works by adding an additional layer of security to your online accounts. It requires an additional login credential – beyond just the username and password – to gain account access

Why should I enable two-step verification?

2FA is an extra step added to the log-in process such as a code sent to your phone. It helps us verify your identity and prevent any unauthorised logins. We value your security and we know the hassle of having to remember passwords.

What do I need to set up two-step verification?

All you need is a mobile phone that can receive SMS messages to enable two-step verification
