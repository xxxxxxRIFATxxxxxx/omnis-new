This is incredibly simple! There are two ways to sell a product on Omnis; Placing an Ask or Sell Now. An Ask is when you place a dollar amount on an item you wish to sell, this means that you are allocating a specific price in which you are willing to sell a specific item. In other words, I am willing to sell X item for $150 e.g. You can set the number of days until an ask expires, the days are 1, 3, 7, 14, 30 and 60. We will notify you in your account section as well as email to let you know that your Ask is about to expire and can be renewed if you wish.

Asks can be updated, or cancelled any time, but it cannot be amended once your item has been purchased or bidded for. It is vital to keep the integrity of all transactions honest and fulfilled, a marketplace is a global 24/7 platform. Integrity and reliability is everything.

The other option is Sell Now. This means you don’t want the hassle of having your item possibly bidded for, it means you want the item sold NOW. When you hit Sell Now, you are agreeing to list your item to be sold for X price immediately.

Congratulations on selling your first item! Once a transaction has been complete, your shipping details including the deadline can be found in the confirmation email we send once your item has been successfully purchased. This can also be found in your My Account section (My account > Selling). You must ensure you print out the shipping label and ship the item (adhering to our shipping standard procedure) prior to the deadline commencing. Most sneakers will have a deadline of two business days to be dispatched, some will have 3-5 depending on the type of release and the brand shipping nature.

The way we see it, we can’t and will not justify charging absurd amounts in transaction fees to our user base simply because we provide a marketplace which brings sellers and buyers to the same platform. What makes sense to myself and the team is we charge low transaction fees to ensure sneakers reflect their true price in the market, not a saturated and inflated price due to sellers getting obliterated with selling fees on other platforms.

We currently ship to 200 countries. Please refer to this list: www.omnisnco.com/countries to see if your country is included. Please note we are actively increasing our list of countries we ship to, please stay tuned for more info.

To ensure the integrity and quality of all items on our website, some items will have no images as they are new releases or we haven’t obtained images that fit our upload quality standards. Please stay tuned as we are always continuing to upload images for all items. .
