This is incredibly simple! There are two ways to purchase a product on Omnis; Placing a bid or Buying It Now

A bid is when you place a dollar amount bid on an item, meaning it is your intention to buy that product for that specific price you bidded, in other words, it’s what you offer for X product. You can select the amount of days before you wish for your bid to expire, the benefit of this is if you set your bidding price just below the lowest ask from a seller, a new seller could come along and be happy to sell his/her item to you for your price. Your bid can last 1, 3, 7, 14, 30 & 60 days. We will notify you in your account section as well as email to let you know that your bid is about to expire and can be renewed if you wish. Bids can be updated, or cancelled any time, but it cannot be amended if it has been successful in purchasing the item. If it does, it’s too late to cancel your bid. We do this because it is vital to keep the integrity of all transactions honest and fulfilled, a marketplace is a global 24/7 platform. Integrity and reliability is everything. Below is an example of all bids placed for X item, it gives you an idea of availability and what others are willing to pay for it. This can give you an indication as to whether your bid amount is too high or too low.

If a seller lists their item and places an ASK that matches the highest bid (assuming you have the highest bid), the item will automatically be sold to you. But, if someone else places a higher bid over you and a seller ASKS for a price that matches the other person, they will win the item. But don’t stress. New sellers can always come along and possibly want to sell it to you for your bidding price. The market is unpredictable, which is why we like it. You never can assume the value of a product, it can change within seconds. Pay attention to the market, don’t be too cheap or too generous, sit somewhere in the middle. The other option is Buy It Now. This means you don’t want the hassle of bidding and possibly winning the item, it means you want the item NOW. When you hit Buy it Now, you are purchasing the item immediately for the lowest ASK.

Due to the nature of a live marketplace, we are unable to offer returns, exchanges or swaps (wrong size, wrong colour etc). The good news is that everything can be resold back onto the platform to a new buyer, as long as the item stays within the selling conditions (deadstock: brand new, never worn).

We understand life is hectic, things can pop up out of nowhere, but this doesn’t stop us from providing everyone on our platform a good experience. If the seller hasn’t shipped his/her item within the allocated time, we will organise a replacement of the exact product from a different seller at no cost to you.

Because we’re a 24/7 global marketplace, we want to ensure we cover a variety of payment options for our buyers. • All Major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, Union Pay and Discover • Debit cards and gift cards • Paypal

Due to the integrity of our platform, we cannot and will not change a shipping address once an order has been confirmed. There are a few different delivery options that our couriers offer, please refer to: www.omnisnco.com/couriers All shipping details on your account can be amended under Account > Settings. Ensure you click “Update” after you’ve amended your details.

There can be several reasons for why an order fails; insufficient funds, listing was removed or the price was changed on the item you were purchasing. Generally, if the order fails while you’ve attempted to buy it, you will not be charged. You will receive an automated error message which will prompt you to try again. Any further issues please reach out to our support team: support@omnisnco.com

If there is a problem with a product you have received on our marketplace, you must contact us within two business days of receiving your item. Please contact us at support@omnisnco.com Provide as much detail as possible on the issue, provide photo evidence and we will ensure all matters are investigated efficiently and thoroughly. If you contact us after two business days of receiving your item, your matter will not be investigated.

All brands will have slightly different sizing standards, we recommend you reach out to that specific manufacturer (Nike, Adidas, Reebok). They can assist you and help recommend the appropriate sizing for you. Gender size conversions are definitely worth noting.

Due to the uniqueness of our buyer to seller platform, most orders will be received within 5-8 business days, (this does not include public holidays or weekends). Most orders can be completed sooner depending on how fast the seller dispatches his item and depending on the courier’s efficiency. Sellers have two business days to dispatch the item once it has been sold, otherwise penalty rates will be applied to the seller. We strive for pride from ourselves, our buyers and sellers.

Don’t stress, it just means your product is sitting at the dock ready to be picked up by one of our partnered shipping carriers. Once the item has been picked up, we will send you a tracking number.
